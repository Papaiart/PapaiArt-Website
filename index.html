Why is it special?

One tool that doesn't make you choose between 2D and 3D

Major industry standards specialise in either 2D or 3D — PapaiArt Animation Studio offers both in a single, focused workflow.

2D

Drawing in 3D space

Dedicated 2D vector engine inside the 3D viewport. Frame-by-frame animation, onion skinning, vector brushes — Grease Pencil-style logic.

3D

Non-destructive modeling

Polygonal mesh editing, Bevel and Knife tools, Mirror and Subdivision Surface modifiers in real time.

IK

Pro-grade rigging tools

Inverse Kinematics, TrackTo, ChildOf, and Limit constraints — complex character animation in a compact package.

▶

Built-in MP4 export

Native H.264 video encoder. Whatever you create on the timeline becomes a shareable video with one click.

⚡

Lightning-fast performance

Optimised real-time engine from the ground up. Instant startup, low memory use, no bloated menus.

⎘

Modular interface

Dockable panels (Inspector, Viewport, Timeline, Hierarchy). Entity-component scene management, infinite Undo/Redo.

2D drawing

A 2.5D look that's only possible here

2D drawings live in real 3D space — with cameras, depth, and lighting. Vector strokes are editable any time, and brushes (Pencil, Pen, Calligraphy, Rough) react to tilt and pressure.

  • Drawing tracks & clips (exposure-sheet logic)
  • Onion skinning with customisable ink colours
  • Independent stroke and fill — flat-design support
  • Drawing Layers panel: lines, shapes, and images as separate sub-layers
3D modeling

Cleanly structured, non-destructive workflow

Tidy hierarchy panel and a real-time modifier stack. Mirror and Subdivision Surface refresh instantly; Bevel and Knife work interactively on the mesh.

  • Polygon mesh editing & topology operations
  • Primitive generators
  • Custom shader and material system
  • Viewport overlays: grid, axes, selection outlines
Render & Export

From the timeline to a shareable MP4 in one click

Real-time viewport renderer. Configurable resolutions, image sequences (PNG), or direct H.264 MP4 encoding — no external editor required.

  • Built-in VideoEncoder with MP4 (H.264) support
  • Image Sequence output (PNG)
  • FHD presets and custom resolutions
  • Render Animation / Render Single Frame at one click
